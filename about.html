Our story

Two book lovers creating more than just a bookstore.

Whisper & Flame was founded by Chloe and Ashleigh for readers who missed the thrill of receiving a book they did not choose themselves, but somehow still felt made for them.

Meet the founders

Chloe and Ashleigh built Whisper & Flame around the magic of surprise.

What began as two friends swapping recommendations, annotating favourites, and hunting through market stalls became a small Australian business with a simple belief: a book can feel like a gift, a ritual, and a little bit of fate.

Chloe and Ashleigh wanted to create more than a place to buy books. They wanted every parcel to feel like stepping into a cosy bookstore where someone knows your favourite tropes, respects your boundaries, and still leaves room for discovery.

Hand-selected books.

Every Blind Date With A Book and curated box is chosen by real readers, not a faceless algorithm. Mood, genre, spice comfort, favourite authors, and hard no topics all shape the recommendation.

A small Australian business.

Whisper & Flame is packed with care in Australia, with practical local shipping, market appearances, and products made for the readers we meet in our own community.

Why it matters
