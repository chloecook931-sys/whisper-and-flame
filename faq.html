FAQ

Everything before your parcel arrives.

Shipping, mystery books, gift notes, and comfort preferences.

How does Blind Date With A Book work?

You choose a genre or mood, then share preferences such as tropes, spice level, favourite authors, and hard no topics. We select and wrap a surprise paperback with clues and extras.

Do you ship across Australia?

Yes. Orders ship Australia-wide, with free standard shipping on orders over $95 AUD. Dispatch is usually 2 to 4 business days unless a product is marked preorder.

Can I send a box as a gift?

Absolutely. Add a gift note at checkout and use the recipient address for shipping. Prices are not included inside the parcel.

Do you include content warnings?

We include comfort notes where possible and encourage customers to add hard no topics. Mystery books still keep the title hidden, but we never ignore safety preferences.

Can I return a mystery book?

Unopened accessories can be returned within 14 days. Because blind date books are curated and wrapped to order, they are not returnable unless damaged or incorrect.
